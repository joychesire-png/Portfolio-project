Hello, I'm Chesire. Someone with a passion for music, philosophy, instruments, soaking up all knowledge I could get because you can never learn too much, right?


I love exploring the depths of ontology and expressing myself through my fashion. And when I'm not lost in thought, you'll find me enjoying the finer things in life, like a great scent, or diving into the world of cinema as a cinephile.

I'm always down to explore new experiences, new restraunts, new music and new places; My current drive for that being this quote by Lester Burnham from the film American Beauty;

"It's hard to stay mad when there's so much beauty in the world. Sometimes I feel like I'm seeing it all at once, and it's too much. My heart fills up like a balloon that's about to burst. And then I remember to relax, and just appreciate the little things. The taste of rain, the smell of the earth, the way the light catches the leaves."
